TECHNICAL & COMPLIANCE

Yes. MoonTrip generates fully GST-compliant invoices that meet all requirements for GST-1 and GST-3B filing for travel agencies.

The Air GST module automatically calculates taxes based on your commission structure for both GDS (Amadeus, Sabre, Galileo) and LCC bookings.

MoonTrip supports dual invoicing — one customer-facing invoice and one internal cost sheet — so your margins stay confidential while your clients see a professional document.

Net profit is calculated by subtracting all service costs from the total quote amount. You can set margins per service and the system aggregates them automatically.

Yes, the payment ledger supports advance payments, partial settlements, and balance carry-forwards specific to the travel industry.

Paste any PNR from Amadeus, Sabre, or Galileo and it automatically parses multi-sector flights into a clean itinerary format.

Set due dates for payment milestones and MoonTrip will automatically remind both you and your clients when payments are upcoming or overdue.

Commissions and discounts are handled separately from the taxable value so your GST reporting remains accurate and compliant.

Cancellations trigger a refund workflow where you can issue partial or full refunds, record cancellation charges, and generate a credit note — all GST-compliant.

The advance ledger tracks every deposit and prepayment per customer, with clear visibility into what's been applied and what's outstanding.
